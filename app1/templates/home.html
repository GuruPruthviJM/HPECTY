About Us

Welcome to the Placement Cell of Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering!!

Our team at the Placement Cell is dedicated to bridging the gap between the students and the industry, and ensuring that our students are placed in reputed organizations across various sectors. Our aim is to provide students with opportunities that align with their skills and interests, and help them kick-start their professional careers.

We work closely with our corporate partners to understand their recruitment needs and help them identify the right talent from our pool of students. Our placement process is designed to facilitate a smooth and seamless transition for both the student and the company. The Placement Cell provides a range of services to students, including resume building, interview preparation, and networking opportunities with industry professionals. Our team is always available to provide guidance and support throughout the placement process.

Over the years, our college has built a strong reputation for producing competent and highly skilled graduates, who are sought after by leading companies. We are proud of our students and their achievements, and we are committed to maintaining our high standards of excellence.

Thank you for choosing VVCE College, and we look forward to supporting you in your career aspirations.
